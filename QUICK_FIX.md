## Still Not Working?

1. **Close Expo Go completely**
   - Swipe away from recent apps
   
2. **Reopen Expo Go**
   
3. **Scan QR code again**
   - Look at terminal where `npm start` is running
   - Use phone camera or Expo Go to scan
